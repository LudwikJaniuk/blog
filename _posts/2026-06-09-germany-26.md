I'm on the way back home after two weeks of travel. The last months included the end of the [disobedient referendum campaign](https://olydigfolkomrostning.se/), [Organize! Movement Meetup](https://www.organizemovementmeetup.org/) in Malmö, and a few other adventures which I'm happy to share about privately upon request. My thoughts currently circle around resistance in times of decline, [cybersecurity for political movements](https://www.systemli.org/), the [limitations and conceptualization of the nonviolent direct action strategy](https://www.grassrootsthinking.com/nonviolence-is-violence-too-somebodys-gotta-die/), as well as the community-building power of eating together. I want to share this [interview series](https://www.arbetaren.se/kategori/bloggar/vfsmg/) where Greta and Alexandra travel around Sweden and interview regular people. 

Best summer wishes to everyone.
